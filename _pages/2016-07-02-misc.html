"The perpetual dialectic". Christmas Presents

I really like to be creative about my Christmas presents (at least that was before I started getting paid...). The most proud I am still about the "match the belly of your reindeer earrings to your mood"-system that I gave my elder sister once, but unfortunately no evidence survives.

  

One year I recorded a 1-hour screencast lecture in which I tried to explain the importance of gauge symmetries in the Standard Model to my family. Cover and thumbnails.

Another year I made a "create a Xmas jingle from your name" that created what I would euphemistically call a "soundscape", based on "Jingle Bells".
